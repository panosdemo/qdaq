\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An L\+ED widget. 

Displays a round or rectangular light emitting diode.

It is configurable to arbitrary colors, the two on/off states and three styles (or \char`\"{}looks\char`\"{});

It may display itself in a performant flat view, a round view with light spot or a round view sunken in the screen.

Joerg Habenicht, Richard J. Moore (\href{mailto:rich@kde.org}{\texttt{ rich@kde.\+org}}) 1998, 1999
